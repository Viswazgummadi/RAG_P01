<<HEADING level=2>>Cross Cut Test
Adhesion<<HEADING_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>The cross-cut test is a simple and easily practicable method
for evaluating the adhesion of single- or multi-coat systems.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<HEADING level=3>>Procedure<<HEADING_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>– Make a lattice pattern in the film with the appropriate
tool, cutting to the substrate
– Brush in diagonal direction 5 times each, using a brush
pen or tape over the cut and remove with Permacel tape
– Examine the grid area using an illuminated magnifier<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>ISO Class.: 2 / ASTM Class.: 3 B
The coating has flaked along the edges and/or at the
intersections of the cuts. A cross-cut area significantly
greater than 5 %, but not significantly greater than 15 %,
is affected.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<HEADING level=3>>Cross-Cut Results<<HEADING_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>Adhesion is rated in accordance with the scale below.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<IMAGE_1>>
[Image 1 contains text: = 3]
<<PARAGRAPH>>ISO Class.: 3 / ASTM Class.: 2 B
The coating has flaked along the edges of the cuts partly or
wholly in large ribbons, and/or it has flaked partly or wholly
on different parts of the squares. A cross-cut area
significantly greater than 15 %, but not significantly greater
than 35 %, is affected.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>ISO Class.: 0 / ASTM Class.: 5 B
The edges of the cuts are completely smooth; none of the
squares of the lattice is detached.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>ISO Class.: 1 / ASTM Class.: 4 B
Detachment of small flakes of the coating at the
intersections of the cuts. A cross-cut area not significantly
greater than 5 % is affected.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>ISO Class.: 4 / ASTM Class.: 1 B
The coating has flaked along the edges of the cuts in large
ribbons and/or some squares have detached partly or
wholly. A cross-cut area significantly greater than 35 %, but
not significantly greater than 65 %, is affected.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>ISO Class.: 5 / ASTM Class.: 0 B
Any degree of flaking that cannot even be classified by
classification 4.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
