or snow, particularly during manoeuvres in port.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<HEADING level=3>>3.2  Visibility<<HEADING_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>Heated glass panes shall ensure perfect visibility in-all
weathers, avoiding the formation of mist or frost, in
relation to the power loading (see Table 5). They shall,
in addition,
ensure
maximum
efficiency
of the
windscreen wipers when operating in conditions of frost
or snow. They shall not cause any significant reduction
in the resolving power of the eye or of binoculars when
a distant object is observed at normal incidence through
the glass.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
