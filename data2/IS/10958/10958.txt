<<PARAGRAPH>>The initial
stage in the design of a joint
is to
determine the conditions
applying to it.
The next
stage is to identify the functions it has in conse-
quence to perform, both those relating
to the
functions
of the joined
components
and
those
resulting
from the presence of the joint as such.
While some functions will
be obviously
relevant
in any particular
situation
in a building,
it
is
necessary, for all criteria to be considered if the
design is to be complete.
Failure in the perfor-
mance of only one required function
may produce
a failure of the joint.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
