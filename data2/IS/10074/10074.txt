<<PARAGRAPH>>0.2 The

It has been recognized

The Sectional

the requirements
of equipment
used for testing
soils to encourage
its development
and manufacture
in the country.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>

dry density
relation

in IS  :  2720 ( Part VII
)-
1980* and IS  :  2720 ( Part VIII
)-1974T.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>

<<PARAGRAPH>>4.3  Base Plate -  The base plate shall be made
from
the same
material
as that of the mould.
The base plate shall have a seating
3 mm deep
on
top face for proper
seating
of mould.
It shall
be square
in shape
and
shall be as detailed
in Fig. 4.
Alternatively,
the base plate shall be made
circular
in shape as detailed
in Fig. 5.
It shall have two tapped
and
two
plain
holes.

tapped
holes
across

or  diameter
shall
be
used

fixing
the
stay
rods
( as shown
in
Fig.
6A
) and
the
plain
holes shall
be used to fix the base plate to the base of an automatic
compactor.
The stay rods shall be fixed to suit the eyes on the mould
and collar
and
four wing
nuts
( as shown
in Fig.
6B ) shall be used to
tighten
the mould
and collar with the base plate.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
