<<PARAGRAPH>>This part of ISO 3506
specifies
the  mechanical properties of nuts made of austenitic, martensitic and ferritic grades
of corrosion-resistant
stainless steels when tested over an ambient temperature range  of 15 “C to 25 ‘C.  Properties
will vary at higher or lower temperatures.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<HEADING level=3>>It  applies to rwts<<HEADING_END>>
<<HEADING level=3>>—
with
nominal
thread
diameters
(d)  up to and including  39 mm;<<HEADING_END>>
<<HEADING level=3>>—
of triangular
ISO
metric

(d) and

in accordance
with
ISO 68-1,
ISO 261
and
ISO 262;<<HEADING_END>>
<<HEADING level=3>>—

<<HEADING level=3>>—
with width
across
flats as specified

<<HEADING level=3>>—

<<HEADING level=3>>It does not apply

<<HEADING level=3>>—

<<HEADING level=3>>—

<<HEADING level=3>>This part of ISO 3506

<<HEADING level=3>>The

<<HEADING level=3>>Corrosion
