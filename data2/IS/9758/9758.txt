<<HEADING level=3>>4. NOMINAL
SIZE<<HEADING_END>>
<<HEADING level=3>>4.1  The nominal
sizes
of the
flush
valves shall
be 15, 25 and 32 mm.
Nominal
size shall be the nominal
bore of the supply
pipe to which
the
valve is connected.<<HEADING_END>>
<<HEADING level=3>>5.  MANUFACTURE
AND CONSTRUCTION<<HEADING_END>>
<<HEADING level=3>>5.1  The flush
valves
of nominal
sizes, 15, 25 and 32 mm
shall have an
outlet of 20, 32 and 40 mm outside
diameter
respectively
and
shall have
threads
conforming
to IS  :  2643 ( Part I )-1975*.<<HEADING_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>5.2 The outlet
of the flush valve shall be provided
with a brass coupling
nut,
so that the flush valve may be connected
to flushing
pipes.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>5.3 The flush valve shall be self-closing
and non-concussive
in action
and
shall be provided
with a push button
or lever for operation.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>5.4 The stop valve having
its principle
of operation
similar
to that of a
fancy
stop
valve
shall
be connected
to the
supply
line.
It shall
be
connected
to the flush valve by a brass coupling.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>

<<HEADING level=3>>6.1 Discharge

-
Discharge

<<PARAGRAPH>>6.2  Discharge
Rate -
When

in IS  :  774-1971*
flush valves shall discharge
at an average rate
of 5 litres
with a tolerance
of plus 0’5 litre in 3 seconds

<<HEADING level=3>>6.3
Working

<<HEADING level=3>>7.  TESTING<<HEADING_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>7.1 Hydraulic

Test -
The
flush
valve

with its

parts shall withstand
an internally
applied hydraulic

of 2 MPa maintained

of 2 minutes

neither
leak nor sweat.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>7.2  Endurance
Test -
The
endurance
test
shall
consist

the valve to 5 000 operations.
It shall then
be
checked
for satisfactory
operation
without
any leakage,
clogging
or failure
of the spring or work-
ing parts.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>7.2.1
One sample of flush valve
picked
at
random
from every batch
of
400
or ,part
thereof

materials,

and
construction
( after

selected
random
sample
for

to 3, 5, 6.1 and 7.1 ).<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
