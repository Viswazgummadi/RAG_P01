<<HEADING level=3>>3. GENERAL
CONDITIONS<<HEADING_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>3.1  Animals
to be transported

They

<<PARAGRAPH>>3.1.1
Animals

<<PARAGRAPH>>3.1.2
Unweaned
puppies or kittens

cage with adult dogs or cats other than their dams.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>3.1.3
Any
dog or cat reported

In extreme
cases:
the animals

qualified
officer.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
