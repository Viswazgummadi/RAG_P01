<<HEADING level=3>>1
Scope<<HEADING_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>This part of ISO 4759 specifies a selection of tolerances for bolts, screws, studs and nuts with ISO metric threads
and with product grades A, B and C and for tapping screws with product grade A.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<HEADING level=3>>NOTE
The product grades refer to the size of the tolerances  where
grade  A is  the most precise
and grade C is the least
precise.<<HEADING_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>The tolerances,
except
tolerances
for threads,
are selected
from the system
of limits and fits specified
in ISO 286-1
and
ISO 286-2.
The
tolerances
for
metric
threads
are
taken
from
the
series
of tolerance
classes
specified
in
ISO 965-3. The tolerances
for tapping
screw threads
are covered
in ISO 1478.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>The
tolerances
of form
and
position
are
specified
and
indicated
in accordance
with
ISO 1101,
ISO 8015
and
ISO 2692.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>The tolerances
specified
in this part of ISO 4759 apply to fasteners
prior to coating
unless
otherwise
specified.
See
also ISO 4042.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>Deviations
from
the tolerances
specified
in this part of ISO 4759
are only
permitted
in product
standards
where
there are valid technical
reasons.
In cases
where
there
is a difference
between
the tolerance
requirements
in this
part of ISO 4759 and the product
standard,
the product
standard
takes precedence.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>It is recommended
that these tolerances
also be used for non-standard
fasteners.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>Dimensions
and tolerances
given in this part of ISO 4759 are in millimetres.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
