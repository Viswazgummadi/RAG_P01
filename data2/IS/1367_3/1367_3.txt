<<PARAGRAPH>>This part of ISO 898 specifies
the mechanical
properties
of bolts, screws
and studs made of carbon
steel and alloy
steel when tested at an ambient
temperature
range of 10 “C to 35 “C.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>Products conforming
to the requirements
of this part of ISO 898 are evaluated
only in the ambient temperature
range
and may not retain the specified
mechanical
and physical
properties
at higher and lower temperatures.
Attention
is
drawn to annex A which
provides
examples
of lower yield stress and stress at 0,2  Y.
non-proportional
elongation
at
elevated temperatures.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>At temperatures
lower than the ambient
temperature
range, a significant
change
in the properties,
particularly
impact
strength,
may
occur.
When
fasteners
are to be used
above
or below
the
ambient
temperature
range
it is the
responsibility
of the user to ensure that the mechanical
and physical
properties
are suitable for his particular
service
conditions.
.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>Certain fasteners
may not fulfill the tensile or torsional requirements
of this part of ISO 898 because of the geometry
of
the head which reduces the shear area in the head as compared
to the stress area in the thread such as countersunk,
raised countersunk
and cheese heads (see clause 6).<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>This part of ISO 898 applies to bolts, screws and studs<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>—<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>with coarse pitch thread
Ml,6
to M39, and fine pitch thread M8 X  1  to M39 X 3;<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>—<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>with triangular
ISO thread
in accordance
with ISO 68-1;<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>—<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>with diameter/pitch
combinations
in accordance
with ISO 261 and 1S0 262;<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>—<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>with thread tolerance
in accordance
with ISO 965-1 and ISO 965-2;<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>—<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>made of carbon steel or alloy steel.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>It does not apply to set screws and similar threaded
fasteners
not under tensile stresses
(see ISO 898-5).<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>It does not specify requirements
for such properties as<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>—<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>weldability;<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
