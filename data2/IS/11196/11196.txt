<<PARAGRAPH>>0.1
This

<<PARAGRAPH>>0.2
The
Indian
Standards
Institution
has already
published
a series
of
standards
on
methods
of testing
soils.
It has been recognized
that
reliable
and
intercomparable

standard

giving
the
desired

accuracy.
The Sectional
Committee
has, therefore,
decided
to bring
out
a series of specifications
covering
the requirements
of equipment
used for
testing
soils
to encourage
its
development
and
manufacture
in
the
country.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>

covered
in this standard
is used
for
determination
of liquid
limit of soil by cone penetration
method
as covered
in IS  :  2720
( Part 5 )-1985*.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>

this

with IS  :  2-19607.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
