<<PARAGRAPH>>This International
Standard specifies the mechanical and performance
properties for prevailing torque type
steel hexagon nuts (including those with flange) when tested over an ambient temperature
range of 10 ‘C to
35 ‘C. Properties will vary at higher and lower temperature.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>

<<HEADING level=3>>- within

<<HEADING level=3>>– within the temperature
range –50 ‘C to +120 “C for non-metallic
insert type nuts’),<<HEADING_END>>
