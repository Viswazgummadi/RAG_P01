<<PARAGRAPH>>3.2  Determinatian

Place the test block
prepared in accordance
with 3.1 under the rod bearing the needle
( C );
lower the needle gently in contact with the surface of the test block and

In the begin-

pierce
the test block.
Repeat

procedure
until the needle, when ‘brought in contact with the test block
and released as described ubove, fails to pierce the block by 5 f 0.5 mm

from the bottom of the mould.
The period elapsing between
the time when water is added to the lime and the time a: which the needle

0.5 mm shall

setting
time.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>

sion on the surface of test block while the attachment
fails to do so shall
be the final setting time.
