<<HEADING level=3>>2.  GENERAL<<HEADING_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>2.1  Description
of Item -The
description

unless

<<HEADING level=3>>2.2  Limits
of Measurement
-
The
dimensions

net

<<HEADING level=3>>2.3  Bills of Quantities
-
The bills of quantities
shall fully describe
the
materials
and
workmanship
and
accura.tely
represent
the
work to be
executed.<<HEADING_END>>
<<HEADING level=3>>2.4  Mode of Measurements
-
The various kinds of builders
hardware
shall
be described
and
measured
separately
according
to the material,
finish, size, pattern
and method
of fixing.<<HEADING_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>2.4.1
All builders
hardware
shall be fully described
and
enumerated
except the following which shall be measured
in running
metres:<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>a) Curtain
rods or poles, stating the outer diameter;<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>b) Curtain
Rails-Curtain
runners,
brackets
and
stops
shall

described
and included
with the item stating
the
number
per
metre of rail;<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>c) Rails for sliding sashes stating the size of the rail; and<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>d) Sash Lines -
The girth or diameter
shall be stated.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>2.5
Fixing
of hardware
items shall include
all fittings,
cutting,
sinking,
boring
and morticing,
the supply of screws ( or bolts, nuts and washers in
the case of hardware
made for fixing with bolts ), to match.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>2.6 Hardware
fixed to wood and metal shall each be measured
separately.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>2.7 Hardware
fixed flush shall be so described.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
