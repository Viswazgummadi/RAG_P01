<<HEADING level=3>>3.  DETERMINATION
OF FINENESS<<HEADING_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>3.1 Procedure -
The  IS Sieves specified in the material specification shall
be superimposed, one above the other with the coarser sieves at the top and
finer sieves at the bottom.
Holding the sieves in both hands, sieving shall be
done with a gentle wrist motion, this will involve no danger of spilling the
lime which shall be kept well spread out on the sieve.
Place 100 g of the
hydrated lime on the top sieve and the material washed through the sieves
with a moderate jet of water from a flexible tube, the whole operation shall
not take more than 30 minutes.
