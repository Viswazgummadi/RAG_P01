<<PARAGRAPH>>This Indian Standard (Sixth Revision) was adopted by the Bureau of Indian Standards, after the drafl finalized
by the Wrought Steel Products Sectional Committee had been approved by the Metallurgical Engineering Division
Council.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>This standard was first published in 1962 and revised in 1969, 1975, 1984, 1992 and 1999. While reviewing
this standard, in the light of experience gained during these years, the Committee decided to revise it to bring in
line with the present practices being followed by the Indian industry and overseas standards of structural steels.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>In this revision the following changes have been made:<<PARAGRAPH_END>>

<<PARAGRAPH>>b) Amendments No. 1, 2, 3 and 4 have been incorporated.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>c) A new clause on references has been incorporated.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>d) Number of grades have been increased to nine.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
