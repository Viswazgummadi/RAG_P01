<<PARAGRAPH>>1. SCOPE<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>1.1  This standard prescribes the requirements
for drinking water trolley
for general purpose.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>2. DESIGN
2.1  The trolley shall be of 250 litres nominal capacity and its dimensions
shall be as shown in Fig. 1.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>3. CONSTRUCTION<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>3.1 The trolley shall consist
of a barrel,
its supports
for mounting
frame,
handle and wheels/castor
for movement.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>3.2 Barrel - The barrel
shall be horizontal
and of cylindrical
shape
with dished or conical ends as agreed to between the purchases and the
supplier.
The diameter of the barrel shall be 500 mm.
The barrel shall
be designed with a positive slope towards its outlet and ensure complete
drainage of the water during the course of cleaning.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>

<<PARAGRAPH>>4.  RUST PREVENTION<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>4.1 The parts liable to rusting shall be painted with an anti-rust
primer
using non-corrosive
filler.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>5.  WORKMANSHIP<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>5.1 The trolley shall be free from warp in structural framework.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>5.2 The welds shall be ground smooth.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>5.3 Wherever,
an opening
in steel sheets is made, suitable stiffening
arrangement
shall be welded near edges of the opening.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>5.4 The painted
surfaces shall be free from wrinkles,
irregular
paint-
ing drippings, scratches and uneven colouring.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>6. TEST<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>6.1 The barrel after grinding the welds and surface
finishing shall be
tested for water leakage by subjecting it to a hydraulic
pressure of 3’5
N/cm2 for 5 minutes.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
