<<PARAGRAPH>>SYMBOLS FOR CONTROLS,
INDICATORS
AND TELL-TALES FOR ROAD VEHICLES<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>1.  Scope-Specifies
the symbols,
that is, conventional
signs, with which certain controls,
indicators
and tell-tales
of a road vehicle
are to be provided
in order to ensure their identification
and facilitate
their
utilization.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<HEADING level=3>>1.1  It also indicates
the colours
of possible
optical
tell-tales
which
warn the driver of the operation
or malfunctioning
of the devices and equipment
connected
to the corresponding
controls.
’<<HEADING_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>1.2 This standard
is applicable
to those controls
which
are fitted on the instrument
panel, or in the
immediate
vicinity
of the driver.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>1.3 This standard
is not applicable
to two-wheelers
and three-wheelers.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<HEADING level=3>>2.  General<<HEADING_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>2.1 The symbols
shall
be as given
in 3, except
for deviations
necessary
to reproduce
an accurate
representation
to the driver’s
line of sight.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>2.2 If, in a symbol,
a vehicle
or parts
of a vehicle
are shown
in a side view, a vehicle
driving
from
left to right shall be assumed.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>2.3 Symbols
on controls
and tell-tales
shall be of a good contrast
to their background
( preferably
light symbol on dark background).<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>2.4 The symbols
and tell-tales
shall
be recognizable
by the driver from his seated position.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>2.5 Focused light shall
be represented
by parallel
rays and diffused
light
by divergent
rays0<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
<<PARAGRAPH>>2.6 If colour
is used on heating
and cooling
systems,
the
colour
red shall be used to indicate
hot
and the colour
blue to indicate
cold.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>

<<PARAGRAPH>>This standard
is in entire agreement
with
IS0 2575-1976 ‘ Road vehicles-symbols
for controls,
indicators
and tell-tales’.
This
standard
aims
at laying
unified
practices
for symbols
for various
controls,
indicators
and tell-tales
for the
user of the road
vehicles.
With
the
publication
and
implementation
of this standard,
it is expected
to eliminate
confusion
for the driver when he happens
to be driving
a vehicle
other
than
his own thus
avoiding
accidents,
introducing
safety
for vehicle
user as well as road user.<<PARAGRAPH_END>>
